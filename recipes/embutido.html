Embutido

Sausage of the Philippines

This food was actually introduced by the spaniards and especially tasty with banana ketchup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of bread crumbs
  • 1 cup of nestle fresh milk
  • 1 kg of ground pork
  • 1 pc of carrot
  • 3 tbsp of pickle relish
  • 1/4 cup of raisins
  • 1 pc of red bell pepper
  • 1 pc of green bell pepper
  • 3 satches of MAGGI magic sarap 8g
  • 3 pcs of hard boild egg
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil

Steps on how to make Embutido

  1. Combine breadcrumbs and fresh milk. Set aside for 5 minutes.
  2. Combine breadcrumbs and milk mixture with ground pork, carrot, pickle relish, raisins and bell peppers. Season with MAGGI MAGIC SARAP® and mix well.
  3. Brush a little oil in aluminum foil and flatten 1 cup of the mixture. Place egg in a row and carefully roll into a log with the egg in the middle. Tie both ends of aluminum foil to tighten.
  4. Steam for 30 minutes and set aside to cool. Unwrap and gently sauté until golden brown. Slice and serve with preferred dipping sauce.
